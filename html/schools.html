Movement & Sound Dance LLC

Movement & Sound Dance LLC is a unique dance school where technique, artistry, and community come together. We work to create a safe, genuinely supportive, and highly creative atmosphere where students of all ages and skill levels can discover or re-discover dance as a positive influence. Classes at Movement & Sound are joyful, both silly and serious, and reflect each instructor's passion for their craft, for teaching children, and his or her individual artistic background.

Studio 83 Dance Academy

Studio 83 Dance Academy is pleased to offer the community high quality, curriculum based dance instruction from professionally trained teachers, with the low cost associated with a park district. We have 4 fully equipped studios with sprung floors, a warm and welcoming waiting area, and a full selection of classes for ages 2 - adult. We invite you all to become a part of our dance family!
